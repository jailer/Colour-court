Fencing

 

We offer a line of high quality chain-link fence desinged for tennis courts

  • -Tennis court chain-link fence systems are commonly used because they're durable & free of maintenance.

     

  • -All steel components or galvanized chain-link tennis court system are hot-dip zinc coated and guaranteed with

    a 12 year coating warranty.

     

  • -Ties used to attach the chain-link fabric to the framework between terminal posts are made from aluminum,

    resulting in maintenance-free.

     

  • -Some fittings used in a tennis court system are made from pressed steel and cast iron for added durability.

     

  • -Outstanding performance in highly corrosive environments provides years of protection.


    -High impact resistance reduces the chance of damage during product handling and installation
    Exceptional resistance to ultra violet light prevents premature fading.

     

  • -Extreme flexibility reduces susceptibility to cracking and chipping, even in changing weather conditions.



 

 

 

 

 

 


Chain Link Fabric

Type:

 

 Polyolefin fused and adhered to zinc-coated steel wire

Gauge:

 

 6, & 9 gauge galvanized core wire

Mesh:

 

 1-3/4"

Heights:

 

 3' -20'

Colours:

 

 Black & Green

Framework

Type 1:

 

 10 mils minimum, over galvanized steel ASMT F 1043, Group 1A, standard weight pipe, schedule 40. Hot-dipped zinc-coated galvanized.

Type 2:

 

 10 mils minimum, over galvanized steel ASTM F 1043, Group 1C, with minimum yield strength of 50,000 PSI. Protective coating per ASTM F 1043, external coating Type B.

Colours:

 

 Black & Green

Top Rail

Type 1:

 

 1-11/16" O.D. Standard weight pipe (0.140" wall thickness, 2.27 lb./ft.)

Type 2:

 

 1-11/16" O.D. DQ 40 pipe (0.111" wall thickness, 1.83 lb./ft.)

Line Posts

Type 1:

 

 2-3/8" O.D. Standard weight pipe (0.154" wall thickness, 3.65 lb./ft.)

Type 2:

 

 2-3/8" O.D. DQ 40 pipe (0.130" wall thickness, 3.12 lb./ft.)

Terminal Posts

Type 1:

 

 3-1/2" O.D. Standard weight pipe (0.203" wall thickness, 5.79 lb./ft.)

Type 2:

 

 3-1/2" O.D. DQ 40 pipe (0.160" wall thickness, 4.64 lb./ft.)
