Tennis Court Surfacing

 



The surfacing system is a textured, durable, all-weather, quick drying colour surface

the life expectancy of asphalt or concrete substrates. Our system is a 100% acrylic

latex intense sports surface formulated with a unique texturing system that allows

you adjust the speed of play. This application can also be used for basketball courts,

play areas and multi-purpose sport surfaces.
